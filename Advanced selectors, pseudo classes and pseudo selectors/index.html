International Red Cross (IRC)

  • We save the injured
  • We help the sick
  • We help the hurt
  • We show humanity

The red cross society was founded by Henri Dunant. He save some people in war between France, Italy and Australia. He and four more men decided to start the red cross to save more people today.

Now the red cross and red crescent society in known all over the world and in almost all contries all over the world. We help the sick, poor and injured. It is a independent society.
